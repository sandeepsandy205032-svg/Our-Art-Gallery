About Our Art Gallery

Project Overview

The Art Gallery website is a creative platform developed to showcase different species of nature such as Birds, Animals, Fishes, and Sea Animals. Each creature is shown with real photographs, an image carousel, descriptions, audio, and videos. We focused on user experience, simplicity, and visual presentation using animations and smooth transitions.

Design Approach

We chose a clean, modern UI with balanced spacing, gradients, and responsive design powered by Bootstrap. The layout is kept simple so users can navigate smoothly and enjoy a museum-like digital experience.
