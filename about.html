About Our Art Gallery

A modern and educational visual gallery showcasing Birds, Animals, Sea Creatures and Nature with high-quality images, structured pages, audio, videos and clean UI design.

How This Idea Started

We wanted to build a clean, organized and visually rich platform where people can explore nature through different categories like Birds, Animals, Fishes and Sea Life.

Every bird or animal has its **own dedicated page** with:

  • Left-side slideshow of multiple images
  • Right-side description & details
  • Audio clips & videos
  • Smooth animations & clear layout
